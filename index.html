About the Baratie

The Baratie is the world famous ocean-going restaurant where you can find fine dining and loads of adventure. Founded by the Legendary Pirate Zeff, the baratie is a paradise for pirates, wanderers, and many others from any corner of the grandline.


Here, food is more than just a meal it's a precious memory and a promise. Every dish is prepared with passion, precision, and the belief that a good meal can change a persons life. From our hearty soups to our seafood masterpieces our menu blends unique flavors with world class culinary skills.


At The Baratie, we welcome everyone equally, marines, pirates, or anyone inbetween and as long as you respect the restaurant and the people dining within our crew of talented chefs will work tireless to deliver hospitality and good meals in the middle of the unprediatable seas.


Whether you're refueling before seeking out your next adventure or seeking a place to grab a warm meal and enjoy a break from the chaos, Baratie will always be there and ready to provide for you.
